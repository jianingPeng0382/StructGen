# Figure Surface Alignment Design

The Structured Data Curation Pipeline and Results sections will use a white section background so their white-backed paper figures visually merge into the page instead of appearing as pasted rectangles. Existing neighboring sections keep their current alternating backgrounds.

Within the paired Pipeline figure, the desktop columns will follow the source-image aspect-ratio relationship (`1fr 2.37fr`) and both images will retain natural height. This aligns their visible top and bottom boundaries without cropping, distortion, or internal letterboxing. Mobile layouts remain naturally sized.

Verification covers the section classes, shared desktop image height, mobile override, existing page tests, and browser measurements.
